Honda CBR 150R Safety Aspects:

Honda CBR 150R is the smallest one in CBR Family, Who comes with standard safety features. From 2019 Honda has added ABS (anti-lock braking system) with this bike Indonesian version. This modern braking system is one of the great safety features. Now the bike also features emergency brake warning, which activates hazard lights in tough situations. Also the bike has strong and adjustable headlight, full-faring body and an oil-cooled engine which ensure a stable ride.
